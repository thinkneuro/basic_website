About us

Think Neuro is dedicated to facilitating health education to communities often overlooked in the educational space. We aim to inspire families to advocate and improve their communal health through delivery of intellectual presentations and sponsorship of professional development opportunities, Intersecting collaboration of highly motivated academics with our emphasis on scholarly research, Think Neuro strives to increase overall scientific literacy and community engagement, particularly among underserved sectors across the US.
